ABOUT ME



Thanks for visiting my site. Besides constantly striving to improve as a web developer, I'm also a musician and overall lover of creative things. I've recently completed a web development bootcamp at General Assembly, and am able to create a wide variety of things using the power of HTML, CSS and Javascript, with some experience in backend development using Node and MongoDB. I've also had experience with several frameworks, including React and Ruby. I look forward to a future of continued learning in this amazing career.
